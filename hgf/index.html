Our Vision

A future where every community has the knowledge, tools and confidence to grow on its own terms.

Heartful Giving Foundation (HGF) exists to bulid a Tanzania where every child living in vulnerable environments lives with dignity, receives protection, quality education and equal opportunities to succeed.

Education

Scholarships, school resourcing and learning support that keep children in the classroom and on track.

Health & Wellbeing

Enhance a condusive environment for development, maternal health outreach and make the needy feel secured and loved.

Youth Empowerment

Encourage, motivate and build a community where youth will be the deciders of the milestones to the community development.

Our Mission

We focus on justfying the vision

To identify, protect and empower children living in vulnerable environments - including orphans, children with disabilities and children with chronic illnesses - through sponsorship, educational support, healthcare services and psychosocialwell-being in collaboration with the community.
