Background & Achievements

From a single tutoring circle in 2023 to a multi-district foundation — here's how HGF has grown, year by year, alongside the communities it serves.

20233 event
The rise of the ogranization

Heartful Giving Foundation official starting point

The co-founders of the organisation wrote a new life chapter by introducing the HGF to the society with the aim of planting a SEED FOR CHANGES

Sanitation

Sanitizing Muhimbili National Hospital - Ward of infants with cancer

The first and historical moment where love and cooperation among the youth with the same mindset germinated into roots for future more moments.

Seed plantation & orphanage visitation

Plating trees to the environment and love sharing

Becoming friend of the nature by planting trees, was an outstanding achievement as an organization and being a source of smile and happiness to young generation in future.

20243 events
Heartful Giving Foundation expansation

New branches establishment

The organization expanded even more further establishing new branches in different corner of Tanzania with the aim of expanding services to more societies in need. The HGF succeeded having 5 branches
(Dar Es Salaam,Morogoro,Dodoma,Tanga and Mbeya).

School

Sanitation and education resource sharing

HGF under Dodoma branch succeeded sharing school resources to some students in one of a secondary school and even more HGF tried to reach female students by facilitating sanitation for them.

Orphanage - Kibaha

HGF Enjoyed with young brothers and sisters at the orphanage and tried to bring back a smile to them.
